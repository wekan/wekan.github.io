Privacy Policy

If you have some data at some WeKan service, data is yours:

  • Your data is not given to any 3rd parties.
  • You can use export functionality or copy-paste to copy your information elsewhere.
  • You can delete your own data using delete functionality. If it seems you are not able to delete your data somewhere, please email support@wekan.fi

For any questions, please email support@wekan.fi . It is email address of Lauri Ojansivu, CEO of WeKan and maintainer of WeKan ® Open Source kanban https://wekan.github.io
